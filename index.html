About

Tekeste Tesfaye B.C & WWC is a private construction-contracting firm established in 1988 by an Ethiopian entrepreneur who has accumulated a wide range of practical experience in the construction sector. The company, with its head office in Diredawa and Addis Ababa town operates throughout the country but mainly in East Hararghe Zone, Oromiya state, Somali state, Diredawa Administrative council, Afar region state, North show Zone ,Axum, Adigudem wereda Tigray zone and Addis Ababa.

Since its establishment, the company was able to successfully complete several buildings construction such as residential blocks, schools (primary, secondary and technical), health centers, clinics, water supply, Gravel road, bridge and irrigation construction projects. As a matter of fact the company had been an active participant to the development efforts being exerted by the Ethiopian Government to improve the welfare and living standards of its people in its area of activity.
